# Business Model Canvas for Code-Scout

## Customer Segments
- Tech professionals (developers, data scientists, and engineers) seeking secure coding insights.
- Companies prioritizing data privacy and local-first solutions for their coding agents.
- Educational institutions and coding bootcamps looking for analytics tools to enhance learning.

## Value Propositions
- Local-first architecture ensuring data privacy and security for sensitive coding projects.
- Advanced search capabilities tailored for coding agents, providing quick and relevant insights.
- Analytics tools that help tech professionals optimize their coding practices and productivity.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the official website and GitHub repository.
- Partnerships with coding bootcamps and tech educational platforms for bundled offerings.
- Online marketing campaigns targeting tech forums, social media, and developer communities.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated customer support via chat and email to assist users with technical queries.
- Community forums for users to share insights, tips, and best practices.
- Regular updates and newsletters to keep users informed about new features and improvements.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: Monthly fee of $29 (approx. 1,000 THB) for individual users.
- Enterprise licensing: Annual fee starting at $5,000 (approx. 175,000 THB) for companies.
- Premium features and add-ons available for purchase, enhancing the core product.

## Key Resources
- Development team skilled in search algorithms and data analytics.
- Infrastructure for hosting the local-first platform securely.
- Marketing and sales team to drive customer acquisition and retention.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and enhancement of search and analytics features.
- Conducting market research to identify emerging trends and customer needs.
- Building partnerships with educational institutions and tech companies.

## Key Partners
- Educational institutions and coding bootcamps for collaborative offerings.
- Cloud service providers for secure infrastructure support.
- Developer communities and forums for marketing and feedback.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: Estimated at $200,000 (approx. 7,000,000 THB) annually for team salaries and tools.
- Marketing expenses: $50,000 (approx. 1,750,000 THB) annually for campaigns and partnerships.
- Infrastructure costs: $30,000 (approx. 1,050,000 THB) annually for hosting and maintenance.
